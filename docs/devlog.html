Raycasting Logic

Raycasting works by projecting rays from the player’s point of view into a 2D map and calculating wall intersections to simulate a 3D scene. Each vertical screen column corresponds to a ray cast into the world.

Development Challenges

One of the hardest challenges was fixing the fish-eye distortion effect. This was solved by correcting the distance calculation using the angle between the player direction and the ray direction.
